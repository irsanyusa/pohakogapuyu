What Emma Watson Hated About the First 'Harry Potter'

November 11, 2015 2:13 PM EST

Emma Watson is synonymous with the Harry Potter movies thanks to her portrayal of Hermione Granger, but the actress says there’s one thing about her character she’s not a fan of: her hair in the first film.

Watson revealed her dislike of Hermione’s hairdo during a magazine shoot for Vogue Italia‘s November issue. “When I see the images of the first Harry Potter, I immediately think of how ugly my hair was,” she told the magazine.

The star also touched on her recent work and focus on equal pay through her campaign HeForShe, saying that she wants men to partner with women “in the walk towards equality.” Watson recently tweeted support for Jennifer Lawrence’s essay on Hollywood sexism and was straightforward with the Guardian when she was asked about gender inequality in the film industry.

“I have experienced sexism in that I have been directed by male directors 17 times and only twice by women,” Watson told the paper. “Of the producers I’ve worked with 13 have been male and only one has been a woman.”
