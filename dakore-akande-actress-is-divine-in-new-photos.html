Actress is divine in new photos

The Nollywood actress took to her Instagram page today, September 8, to share stunning new photos of herself.

Dakore who is currently in Los Angeles, looked regal rocking a pink head wrap and a colourful material in the traditional 'buba' style unique to the Yoruba's.

Since her return to the movie industry, Dakore has been garnering attention both with her skill on the TV screens as well as her re-branded personality.
