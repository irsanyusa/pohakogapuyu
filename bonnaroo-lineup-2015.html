Bonnaroo 2015 Announces Line-up | TIME

January 13, 2015 10:25 PM EST

The Bonnaroo Music and Arts Festival has announced its 2015 line-up, with piano legend Billy Joel, Kendrick Lamar, Deadmau5 and Mumford and Sons set to headline.

The festival takes place over four days from June 11 to 14 at Great Stage Park in Manchester, Tennessee, and tickets go on sale Jan. 17.

Other hot names include the War on Drugs, SBTRKT, Unknown Mortal Orchestra, Jamie XX, Bahamas and Royal Blood.
