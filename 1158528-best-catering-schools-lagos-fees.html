How much is a catering school in Lagos?

Culinary and catering schools in Lagos charge between N20,000 and N3,000,000, depending on the program and the training institute.

How can you become a Chef in Lagos?

Get culinary and catering training and certification from an accredited institute within or outside Nigeria. As a graduate, you can apply for jobs such as Line Cook, Sous Chef, Caterer, and Personal Chef in Lagos.

What are the qualifications for a chef?

You need a culinary arts certificate, diploma, degree, or equivalent professional qualifications. You should also have excellent knowledge of current culinary trends and dining best practices, creative culinary skills, and a passion for food and culinary arts.

What is the first thing to learn as a chef?

A professional chef should master knife skills, learn to make Five Mother Sauces and perfect sauces, and be knowledgeable about food and kitchen hygiene and safety.
