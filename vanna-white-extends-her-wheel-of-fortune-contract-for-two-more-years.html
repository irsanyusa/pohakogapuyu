Vanna White Extends Her 'Wheel Of Fortune' Contract For Two More Years

TALENTRECAP.COM Entertainment Celebrity

ABC/Christopher Willard

Reading Time: 2 minutes

Longtime Wheel Of Fortune co-host Vanna White has just extended her contract for two more years. She’ll continue revealing those famous letters on the puzzle board through the 2025-2026 season. As one chapter closes, another begins. In the fall of 2024, Ryan Seacrest will step into the shoes of retiring host Pat Sajak, who has been at the helm since 1981.

Vanna White Extends Her Wheel Of Fortune Contract

Pat Sajak’s decision to retire after 41 seasons marked the end of an era for Wheel Of Fortune. The show, which first hit the airwaves in 1975, has been a staple of American television for decades, bringing smiles and prizes to countless households. In June, Sajak announced his retirement, leaving fans wondering about the future of the beloved show.

Thankfully, White, who has been Sajak’s co-host since December 1982, is here to provide some continuity. Her decision to extend her contract ensures that viewers will still get to see her in action as she turns those letters and adds to the excitement of the game. Terms of White’s new contract haven’t been disclosed, but her dedication to the show and the fans is crystal clear.

Meanwhile, as we bid farewell to Sajak, we can’t help but look forward to the arrival of Seacrest. Known for his work on shows like American Idol and Live with Kelly and Ryan, Seacrest expressed his excitement at joining the Wheel Of Fortune family. His infectious enthusiasm promises to bring fresh energy to the show.

As Seacrest takes the reins of Wheel Of Fortune, he acknowledges the pressure that comes with stepping into a legendary host’s shoes. But he’s also ready for the fun and thrill of giving away prizes every night, a sentiment shared by countless fans of the show.

Ryan Seacrest Excited to Team Up with Vanna White on Wheel of Fortune

As the countdown continues for Seacrest to take the reins of Wheel Of Fortune in the fall of 2024, he’s not holding back his excitement about working alongside White. In a candid interview, Seacrest expressed his elation and admiration for White.

Seacrest acknowledged that White has been a staple in viewers’ living rooms for many years, and he couldn’t contain his enthusiasm for their upcoming partnership. While Seacrest is set to usher in a new era of Wheel Of Fortune, he recognizes the importance of having White by his side.

“This is such great news,” he said. “I’ve been very excited to work with her, but now that it’s official, I can say, Congratulations, Vanna. I can’t wait.”

He emphasized her beloved status and the comfort she brings to viewers, which will undoubtedly ease his transition into his new hosting role.
