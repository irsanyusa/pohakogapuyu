On Tuesday night, FOX aired a special two-hour episode of The Masked Singer featuring a holiday sing-along. Several contestants from Season 10 of the show performed holiday classics ahead of Wednesday’s Group C Finals. This is the last week of the competition before next week’s finale.

The Masked Singer Season 10 Airs Holiday Sing-Along

During Tuesday’s sing-along, we got to hear several of this season’s remaining contestants perform festive holiday songs. A couple of unmasked celebrities from this season also performed, including Luann De Lesseps (Hibiscus) and Tyler Posey (Hawk).

Gazelle Sings ‘Underneath the Tree’

Gazelle recently earned the “Ding Dong, Keep It On” Bell from the panelists, saving them from elimination in the Group A Finals and sending them to next week’s finale. For the holiday sing-along, Gazelle sang Kelly Clarkson’s song “Underneath the Tree.”

Cow Sings ‘What Christmas Means to Me’

Along with Gazelle, Cow made it through the Group A Finals and into the finale. For Tuesday’s special, they took the stage to sing “What Christmas Means to Me” by Stevie Wonder from a reindeer-driven sleigh.

Sea Queen Sings ‘Winter Wonderland’

During last week’s Group B Finals, Sea Queen reigned supreme, securing a spot in the Season 10 finale. For the holiday sing-along, they performed a jazzy rendition of “Winter Wonderland” by Ella Fitzgerald.

Donut Sings ‘Frosty the Snowman’

We’ll have to wait until Wednesday’s Group C Finals to find out if Donut makes it to the Season 10 finale. In the meantime, enjoy their performance of “Frosty the Snowman” by Gene Autry. The stage was even decorated with donut snowmen!

Luann De Lesseps Sings ‘What Do I Want for Christmas’

Earlier this season, Hibiscus was unmasked as Real Housewives of New York City star Luann De Lesseps. During Tuesday’s special, she took the stage unmasked to perform her own holiday song, “What Do I Want for Christmas.”

Tyler Posey Sings ‘Jingle Bell Rock’

De Lesseps wasn’t the only previously unmasked celebrity to perform during Tuesday’s special. Hawk, who was unmasked as Tyler Posey earlier this season, delivered a fun rendition of “Jingle Bell Rock” by Bobby Helms.
