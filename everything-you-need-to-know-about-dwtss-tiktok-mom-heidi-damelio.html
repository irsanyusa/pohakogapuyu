Everything You Need to Know About 'DWTS's TikTok Mom Heidi D'Amelio

From starring in her own family reality show on Hulu to being a mom of two world-famous TikTok stars. Here’s everything you need to know about TikTok mom and Dancing With The Stars contestant Heidi D’Amelio.

Heidi is an American internet personality and social media influencer. She came to public prominence after the D’Amelio family’s Hulu reality show, The D’Amelio Show. Known as the mother of Charli and Dixie, Heidi is also a TikTok star.

Heidi has secured her place as an internet celebrity mom with over 10 million followers on TikTok and more than two million followers on Instagram. Though her daughters both post dance and music content, she mainly shares fashion, fitness, lifestyle, and family videos.

She Truly Rose to Fame Alongside Her Daughters on Their Reality Show

Although millions of people have seen Charli and Dixie’s faces, not many fans knew their parents ahead of the family reality show. Heidi and her husband Marc D’Amelio appear alongside their daughters in their ongoing reality series. The D’Amelio Show mainly documents the challenges and opportunities the family faces after their daughters skyrocketed to fame.

Within the series, Heidi is seen as the mother that keeps the family together through all the constant changes and adjustments. Even though the D’Amelio family will never be the same following Charli and Dixie’s overnight fame, Heidi keeps the girls grounded.

The main focus of the show is the family adapting to Hollywood limelight. The show started back in September 2021 and is getting renewed for a second season premiering in September year.

She is Competing in the Next Season of DWTS Along with Daughter Charli

Heidi and her daughter Charli have officially been confirmed for the Dancing With the Stars season 31 cast. In a riveting mother-daughter fashion, they will be competing against each other for this year’s Mirrorball trophy.

During Wednesday morning’s Good Morning America announcement, Heidi revealed that she spent her childhood competitively dancing, just like her daughter. For those who may not know, Her daughter Charli was a competitive dancer for over ten years, eventually finding success in sharing her talent on TikTok.

Both Charli and her mom Heidi have a sizeable online presence, and their fans are excited to see their appearance on the show. Whichever of the two of them comes out on top, this is surely a much-awaited season because of their presence.
