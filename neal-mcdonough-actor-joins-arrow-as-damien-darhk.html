Actor joins 'Arrow' as 'Damien Darhk'

Set to appear in the upcoming fourth episode, McDonough plays Darhk, former colleague of the late Ra’s al Ghul, who briefly visited Starling City in the season three finale.

According to the show’s producers, Darhk's character as “a  mysterious and sophisticated villain, and an adversary of the late Ra’s al Ghul. Now the leader of his own clandestine group, this cunning foe will prove to be one of the Arrow’s greatest challenges.”
