Jax Teams Up With Simple Plan For Re-Release Of Their Hit 'Iconic'

Reading Time: 3 minutes

Pop sensation Jax and Canadian rock band Simple Plan have come together for a fresh take on the hit track “Iconic.” Originally featured on Simple Plan’s Harder Than It Looks album, this collaboration adds a new dimension to the song while shining a spotlight on Jax.

Jax Collaborates With Simple Plan On a New Music Video

The music video for “Iconic” is a thrilling compilation of footage from Simple Plan’s dynamic live shows during their tour with fellow rock acts The Offspring and Sum 41. Jax, known for her pop music and her impressive following on TikTok, expressed her enthusiasm about the collaboration.

“I was so, so, so excited when Simple Plan asked me to be part of ‘Iconic!’ Pierre brought me up to his home, and we had such a fun time cutting my verse and then having dinner with his beautiful family.” Jax said. “I grew up listening to them and was trying my best not to fangirl. The boys are all Icons, and it is a dream come true to be on this song with them.”

“That’s why we thought Jax was the perfect artist to join us on the song.” Pierre Bouvier, the lead singer of Simple Plan, added. “She embodies everything ‘Iconic’ stands for. She’s forging her own path, breaking rules, and doing things her way, and it’s been cool to watch her use her platform in such a positive and inspiring way. We couldn’t be happier to be friends and have her on this song.”

The collaboration between Jax and Simple Plan showcases what happens when artists from different genres come together. Their partnership creates a new life for “Iconic” and adds a fresh perspective to the track.

Jax May Appear On Simple Plan’s Tour

In addition to their exciting collaboration, there’s more thrilling news on the horizon for fans of Jax and Simple Plan. The rock band is gearing up for The Hard As Rock Tour across Europe. This tour kicks off at the beginning of next year.

This eagerly awaited tour will not only feature Simple Plan, it will include support from Mayday Parade and State Champs. The three groups are definitely set to perform a show of a lifetime for fans across the continent.

What makes this tour even more remarkable is the band’s commitment to making a positive impact. Simple Plan has committed to donating $1 from each ticket sold to the Simple Plan Foundation. The Foundation is a charitable organization they founded in 2005.

This foundation dedicates itself to supporting young people in need. It also emphasizes the role of music as a tool to discover purpose in life. Over the years, the Simple Plan Foundation has made substantial contributions to various charitable causes in Canada. As Jax and Simple Plan come together for this iconic collaboration, their shared commitment to giving back adds an extra layer of significance to their musical journey.
