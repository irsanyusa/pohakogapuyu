Wynonna Judd's 10 Most Iconic Songs of All Time

TALENTRECAP.COM Entertainment Celebrity

Photo by Terry Wyatt/Getty Images

Reading Time: 2 minutes

Wynonna Judd is all set to make a grand entrance in the ongoing season of The Voice as the mega mentor. In celebration of her incredible musical journey, we’ve compiled a list of Wynonna’s top 10 most iconic songs that have solidified her status as a female country music legend.

Wynonna Judd’s Top 10 Best Songs of All Time

10. “I Want to Know What Love Is” Featuring Jeff Beck

In this powerful cover with the Yardbirds guitar player Jeff Beck, Judd’s vocals shine as she brings her signature style to a classic rock ballad.

9. “Love Is Alive”

In this single from The Judds’ debut album, Why Not Me, the singer infuses a dose of both rock and blues into her country sound. The result is a soulful track that shows her range as an artist.

8. “Is It Over Yet”

With “Is It Over Yet,” Judd explores themes of heartbreak and resilience. Her soulful delivery and the song’s emotional depth touches fans from all walks of life.

7. “Girls with Guitars”

Empowering anthem, “Girls with Guitars” celebrates female artists who break barriers in the music industry. Judd’s strong vocals and the song’s message certainly make it a standout track.

6. “Tell Me Why”

The title track from her second solo album, “Tell Me Why,” is a heartfelt ballad that shows off both Judd’s emotional depth and storytelling abilities.

5. “Rock Bottom”

A dynamic and energetic track that infuses rock elements into Judd’s country sound, “Rock Bottom” resonates with fans for both its raw emotion and powerful vocals.

4. “Mama He’s Crazy” With The Judds

As one-half of The Judds, Wynonna delivered this country music classic with her mother, Naomi, showcasing their harmonious blend of voices and catchy melody.

3. “She Is His Only Need”

This song established Judd as a solo artist, and it’s no wonder why. Both the heartfelt lyrics and her commanding vocals create a beautifully evocative experience for listeners.

2. “I Saw the Light”

A gospel-infused track, “I Saw the Light” reveals Judd’s versatility as an artist. Her rendition of this timeless classic is both soul-stirring and moving.

1. “No One Else on Earth”

Judd’s chart-topping hit “No One Else on Earth” is a showcase of her emotional depth and vocal prowess. The song’s passionate lyrics and memorable melody have certainly made it a fan favorite.
