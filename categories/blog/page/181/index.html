How are global consumers finding new music?

published on July 09, 2024
Traditional ways of discovering the latest music – hearing something on the radio, getting a recommendation from a friend or catching an earworm from a movie – remain important. But video games, commercials, and social media are crucial in capturing the attention of the world’s young consumers. Overall, among consumers in 17 markets in which YouGov conducted research, music apps such as Spotify are the most popular way to discover new tunes (36%) as more consumers migrate to streaming apps.
