Mamma mia! Season 11 of The Masked Singer is off to an impastably good start, with two epic reveals so far and a ton of insane performances. But this week, Spaghetti & Meatballs joins the competition to try to add his secret sauce to Billy Joel Night.

But who is under the Spaghetti & Meatballs Mask? Is it really a celebrity chef or is that just to throw us off? Let’s go through all the clues we have so far and break them down to make our prediction.

Clues on The Masked Singer Season 11

Prior to his first appearance on The Masked Singer, we did get some clues on Instagram about the celebrity under the mask. His clues were a chef hat, a sign that said “BANC,” him flipping a baguette, a red sports car, and salt and pepper shakers. Plus, some fans have caught on that he might’ve done a fist pump at the end of the clues video!

Popular Guesses for The Masked Singer Spaghetti & Meatballs

Even with minimal clues and one performance, the guessing game is going strong in the comments! The most popular predictions include John Travolta, Billy Joel himself, or a celebrity chef. Some of the top guesses for chefs are Bobby Flay, Guy Fieri, or Joe Bastianich from MasterChef.

Who is Spaghetti & Meatballs on The Masked Singer?

We don’t have enough to go off of to make a prediction quite yet, but I personally really like the Joe Bastianich guess. Plus, MasterChef commented on the Instagram video of his first performance! Could that be a clue in itself or just some shameless FOX self-promotion?
