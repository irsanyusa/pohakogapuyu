Ranking Niall Horans Best Fashion Looks on The Voice

TALENTRECAP.COM Singing The Voice

Evans Vestal Ward/NBC

Reading Time: 3 minutes

Since gracing The Voice in early 2023, Niall Horan has been serving fans not only with his humor, but also his distinct sense of style. From his cozy jackets to his dapper cropped jeans, the pop singer is clearly an irresistible fashion silhouette.

Niall Horan’s Best Looks on The Voice

8. Olive Green Collared Sweater

During The Voice Season 23 Battle Rounds, Horan kept it casual with an olive green collared sweater, that he layered on with a white shirt. He matched this piece with a pair of black denim pants and black loafers. Then, to complete the look, he accessorized himself with small pieces of silver jewelry.

7. Denim on Denim

Horan is definitely not shying away from sporting the Canadian tuxedo. In another Season 23 Battle Round episode, the pop singer slipped on a short-sleeved denim shirt to pair with his denim jeans. This whole ensemble gave off a dashing vibe, a stylish clean look for the new coach.

6. Sheer Green Knit Top

During the Season 23 Playoffs, Horan decided to wear an outfit with a pop of color. Wearing a pair of ivory pants and white shirt, Horan completed the ensemble with a sheer plain green knit top. This is perhaps one of his most effortless yet slick looks on the show.

5. Dark Brown Suit

For the Season 23 Finale, the pop singer went for a beautiful dark brown suit. Underneath, Horan was wearing a light blue silky shirt to make entire look two-toned. To complete the outfit, Horan wore a pair of black shoes.

4. Elephant Print Vest

One thing about Horan’s fashion style is that he can look cute and manly at the same time. For his Season 24 Battle Rounds outfit, Horan wore a vest embroidered with elephant icons on it. He used the same to layer his basic white tee and and complement his navy blue pants.

3. Light Taupe Vest

In another one of his Season 24 Battle Rounds episodes, Horan slipped on a plain light taupe vest on his white tee. He’s made the outfit a little cowboy-ish by donning a good pair of light-washed denim jeans. This look is simple but has nonetheless given an edgy vibe.

2. Ivory Suit

During the Season 23 Live Shows, Horan was donning a luxurious ivory suit to seize the exciting night. The new coach paired the dazzling terno with a vertically striped shirt underneath. With his usual silver accessories, this look stunned on the singer.

1. Bejeweled Suit

The Season 23 Finale marked the end of Horan’s first season on The Voice, and he obviously honored the special occasion by wearing a ravishing suit. For the night, he wore a matching black top and pant set bejeweled with multicolored flowers. It’s a no-brainer — it’s his best outfit on the show as of writing.
