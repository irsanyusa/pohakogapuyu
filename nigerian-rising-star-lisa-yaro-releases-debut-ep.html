Nigerian rising star Lisa Yaro releases debut EP

The EP has six tracks and is the resplendent product of a perfect upbeat blend of the African five-pattern beat rhythm and R&B. Its release was led by ‘Savage’, a vibrant party anthem released earlier in May 2023. In ‘Savage’ Lisa unmasked how much energy she could exude in a record, and it plays out as well in the performance of most songs on ‘My Way’.

Lisa Yaro – Domitilla, Feat. Mayorkun

Preceding her debut project, Lisa has always been known for her magical songwriting; having collaborated with renowned names like Will. I.Am, LeToya Luckett and Mya on the Grammy-nominated project ‘Smoove Jones’ in the Best R&B Album category. And in a bid to tap into even more markets, Lisa has employed some elements of African pop music and Nigerian fusions in some songs of ‘My Way’.

As an independent solo artiste, Lisa Yaro doesn’t seem to be slowing down when it comes to breaking new grounds in the entertainment industry, as her music continues to air on essential media including New York’s Hot97, SoundCity and Cool FM; the latter two based in Lagos, Nigeria.

Moreover, her hard work and resilience are evident in how she has been internationally recognized while performing at the 2022 AFRIMMA Awards in Dallas, Texas; owning the stage and stealing the hearts of her audience with oomph storytelling.

Lisa’s music can only be described as uber-creative and appropriately melodic. Some of her recent singles include ‘Yaro’ – which features Nigerian street pop artiste, Zlatan – and ‘Biko’ – a record that fits all seasons.

Lisa Yaro – YARO, Feat. Zlatan

Most media outlets including BET Jams, Revolt TV and BET show have described her music as “a unique reflection of her cultural heritage…” or something close to that, courtesy of The Knockturnal music blog.

At the end of the day, above everything else, Lisa Yaro is here to stay and ‘My Way’ is the stone that both kills the bird of showcasing how incredibly talented she is and cementing her place in the music industry at a global level.
