After a riveting round of blind auditions that left audiences on the edge of their seats, the stage is now ready for an exhilarating season of The Voice Australia. The suspense has finally come to an end as the teams for season 12 have been unveiled, bringing together a diverse array of musical talents under the guidance of four accomplished judges.

The Voice AU Revealed the Complete Members of Each Team

Rita Ora takes the reins of her team with a touch of sophistication. Known for her ability to connect deeply with artists and push them beyond their limits. With contestants like Ben Esber, Levi X, Gezel Bardossi, Tarryn Stokes, Nick Cunningham, Nyree Huyser, Sihana Haxhnikaj, Cruize Karaitiana, Gabby Asta, Elsa Marilyn, Brenda Bressed, and Emily Kate, Ora’s team showcases a blend of incredible potential.

As a seasoned presence on The Voice AU, Guy Sebastian certainly brings extensive experience to mentor and guide his handpicked talents. His team includes Shanae Watson, Overnight, Bella Mackenzie, Michaela Jayde, Robbie Hunt, Maz Green, Jaxson Cornell, Marley Sola, Elly Poletti, Jaydean Miranda, Libby Worboys, and Charlie Pittman.

The international sensation Jason Derulo graces the panel with his star power, seamlessly merging diverse genres and also captivating audiences worldwide. His team boasts a lineup of talent including Etienne Steven, Ethan Beckton, Andrew Taylor Knight, Calista Nelmes, Charlette Ginu, Maree Mamalis, Alex Jeans, Caitlyn Bamber, Tee & Raye, Jade Talbot, Christian Ellis, and Callum Warrender.

Jessica Mauboy, a beloved figure in Australian music known for her versatile voice and also her infectious charisma, offers her unique perspective as a judge. Her team, an eclectic mix of talents, comprises Ezra Williams, Tiarose Burgess, Dillon Rhodes, Sean Millis, David Aumua, Giaan Jordan, Jade Taunton, The Bushwackers, Trisha Godinet, Shyjana, Dan Daniels, and Nenah Jones.

Fans Speculate Which Team Will Win

As the anticipation for the latest season of The Voice AU reaches its peak, fans have already begun to speculate about which judge is most likely to lead their team to victory. With the final twelve contestants yet to be chosen, viewers are then turning to Sportsbet to place their bets on the potential winner of the competition.

Interestingly, many fans are choosing to back the judge they believe has the best shot at taking their contestant all the way to the winner’s circle. Last year’s triumphant coach, Ora, who guided PE teacher Lachie Gill to victory, is a favorite once again, boasting odds of $2.00. Close on her heels is the new international addition, Derulo, with odds of $2.25. Surprisingly, despite Australia’s own musical talents Sebastian and Mauboy having won the crown in previous seasons, they find themselves trailing with odds of $6.00.

Adding more fuel to the anticipation, recent episodes have shown the judges dividing into rival camps as the competition intensifies. In a playful yet competitive exchange, Australian judges Sebastian and Mauboy expressed their determination not to let the international judges, Derulo and Ora, snatch the crown this year.
