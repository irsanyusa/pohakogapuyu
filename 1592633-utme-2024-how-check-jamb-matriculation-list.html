UTME 2024: How to Check Your Name on JAMB Matriculation List

  • Verifying the name of a UTME candidate on the JAMB matriculation list is an important step in an admission process
  • The JAMB matriculation list confirms the legitimacy of candidates' admission and protects them from fraudulent schemes
  • The list is a compilation of students who have received admission offers from tertiary institutions in Nigeria

Legit.ng journalist Adekunle Dada has over 5 years of experience covering basic and tertiary education in Nigeria and worldwide

FCT, Abuja - After the Joint Admissions and Matriculation Board (JAMB) released the 2024 Unified Tertiary Matriculation Examination (UTME) results, candidates are seriously waiting for the release of cut-off marks, which would determine their admission status for the 2024/2025 session.

UTME candidates who are successfully offered admission will find their names on the JAMB matriculation list.

What is JAMB matriculation list?

The JAMB matriculation list keeps record of students who have received admission offers from tertiary institutions in Nigeria. Hence, candidates need to ensure their names are on the JAMB matriculation list.

Only UTME candidates with admission offers from recognized institutions are listed on the JAMB matric list.

Checking your name on JAMB matriculation list

Verifying your name on the JAMB Matriculation List is an important step in securing the admission process into tertiary institutions in Nigeria.

The first step is to verify that your name is on the JAMB matriculation list by visiting the JAMB matriculation list portal on the e-Facility website.

The second step is for the UTME candidates to enter their JAMB registration number and examination year

The candidate's name will be displayed if it’s on the JAMB matriculation list.

Getting your name on JAMB matriculation list

To ensure the name is on the JAMB matriculation list, the UTME candidate must access and print his/her admission letter (if/when it’s available) from the e-Facility Portal.

The next stage is for the UTME candidate to obtain and print the JAMB result slip (if/when it’s available) via the e-Facility platform.

UTME candidates are advised to seek confirmation from their institutions' admissions officer regarding the Admission Letter and JAMB result slip.

Importance of JAMB Matriculation list

The JAMB Matriculation List helps to confirm the legitimacy of the admission and shields UTME candidates from fraudulent schemes.

NYSC mobilisation: The JAMB matriculation list serves as one of the important requirements to qualify for the National Youth Service Corps (NYSC).

Validity of admission: The list validates the authenticity of the admission and the accreditation status of the institution by the JAMB.
