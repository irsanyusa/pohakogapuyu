Africa's richest man, Dangote shows off the billion Dollar dance at daughter's wedding

Africa's richest man, Aliko Dangote was caught dancing to the tunes of Afro-pop star, Davido as he performed to guests that attended his daughter's wedding.

As Davido found his way to the side of the man, whose net worth as estimated by Forbes in 2018, was put at $14.1billion, Dangote was spotted dancing to Davido's Fia as guests sang along.

On Saturday, March 24, 2018, Dangote held a lavish wedding reception for his beautiful daughter, Fatimah and her husband, Jamil Abubakar.

The wedding reception held inside the Conference Centre of the Eko Hotel and Suites.

Dangote has always been a fan of the singer from the inception of his musical career in 2011.

The billionaire and chairman of Dangote group was one of the special guests at the album of launch of Davido's first album, Omo Baba Olowo.

He had also attended some of the shows where the singer had performed alongside other notable Nigerian musicians.
