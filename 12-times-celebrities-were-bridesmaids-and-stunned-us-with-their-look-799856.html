12 Times Celebrities Were Bridesmaids and Stunned Us With Their Look

Taking a back seat for your best friend’s wedding can be a tough job, especially when you’re a celebrity who’s always the center of everyone’s attention. The heroes of our article are gorgeous celebrities who agreed to play second fiddle, but still looked stunning in their bridesmaid gowns. And yes, some of them could not escape wearing matching dresses and similar hairdos.

Here at Bright Side, we found 12 gorgeous gowns that celebrities were wearing when they were honored to be someone’s bridesmaid, and here they are.

Cara Delevingne

In 2014, Cara Delevingne was a bridesmaid at her sister Poppy’s wedding. She chose a white dress with an open back and short ruffled sleeves, and topped up her look with black and white heeled sandals. Cara chose to not wear jewelry which made her look even more exquisite.

Eva Longoria

Eva Longoria’s outfit at her friend’s wedding in 2015 was similar to the gowns the other bridesmaids were wearing, but it didn’t make Eva look any less fabulous. The long, seafoam green, strapless dress fit her just perfectly.

Emily Ratajkowski

The square neckline dress worn by Emily Ratajkowski has no unnecessary details and looks quite laconic. The caption of this pic was short and simple too: “Bridesmaids,” wrote Emily on her Instagram when posting this wedding photo.

Paris Hilton

The gauzy gown in a pastel blue color made Paris Hilton look like royalty at her sister Nicky’s wedding to James Rothschild that took place at Kensington Palace, in London in 2015.

Pippa Middleton

The outstanding gowns that the guests and participants of the Royal Wedding ceremony were wearing made it an unforgettable event. The dress of Pippa Middleton is no exception. The cream-colored dress that fit her like a glove looked exquisite and thrust the bride’s sister into the spotlight.

Beyoncé

In 2014 Beyoncé attended the wedding of her sister Solange Knowles. The singer chose a simple white dress and eye-catching shoes to top off her look that day.

Sofia Vergara

In 2015 Sofia served as a bridesmaid for one of her friends. She was wearing a pale yellow strapless dress which perfectly matched the wedding gown of the bride.

Nina Dobrev

In 2017 Nina Dobrev was a bridesmaid at her friend’s wedding and she was wearing a long “dusty” pink strapless dress with little flowers on top.

“Oh and did I mention that this was the most fun wedding I’ve ever been to?,” wrote Nina on her Instagram post. And it looks like she did have a lot of fun!

Rachel Bilson

Rachel Bilson’s bridesmaid dress with a long train and sweetheart neckline looks like it came right off the runway. The “Dusty” pink hue of the dress doesn’t steal the show and makes Rachel look gorgeous.

Victoria Beckham

Black is a rare color choice for a wedding, but Victoria Beckham looked gorgeous in her strapless form-fitting black dress when she played the role of Eva Longoria’s bridesmaid in 2016.

Vanessa Hudgens

In 2019, Vanessa was a bridesmaid at her friend Kim Hidalgo’s wedding. Vanessa was wearing a matching bridesmaid dress in grey that was covered in a beaded floral motif.

Keira Knightly

In 2011, actress Keira Knightly played the role of a bridesmaid at her brother Caleb’s wedding. She wore a bouffant skirt dress in a deep purple color and held a flower bouquet in her hands.

In her interview with Jimmy Fallon, Keira confessed that she thought she was “a really bad bridesmaid.” “I kept dropping things all over the dress and every drink I had went straight down the front, and then the food went straight down the front. I’m never gonna be asked to be a bridesmaid again,” said the actress, laughing.

Have you ever been a bridesmaid? What were you wearing? Show us the pictures of you in your bridesmaid gowns in the comments!
